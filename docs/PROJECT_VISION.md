# InsightPulse

## Vision

InsightPulse is a Financial Market Intelligence & Decision Analytics Platform built on top of AlphaLens.

While AlphaLens focuses on collecting financial news, analyzing sentiment, and generating market signals, InsightPulse focuses on transforming that intelligence into explainable, actionable, and business-oriented market insights.

The platform is designed to help analysts, investors, researchers, and decision-makers prioritize information, identify emerging opportunities and risks, understand market behavior, and support data-driven decision making.

Rather than attempting to predict stock prices, InsightPulse focuses on answering one critical question:

> **"What deserves attention right now, why does it matter, and what evidence supports it?"**

---

# Real-World Problem

Financial professionals are exposed to an overwhelming volume of market news every day.

Reading hundreds of headlines, tracking multiple companies, understanding changing market sentiment, comparing price behavior, and identifying important developments manually is time-consuming and inefficient.

Existing dashboards often show charts and statistics but fail to answer:

* What actually changed?
* Why is it important?
* Which companies require immediate attention?
* What opportunities are emerging?
* What risks should be investigated?

InsightPulse addresses this problem by automatically converting raw financial news into structured intelligence, explainable analytics, and actionable insights.

---

# Core Objectives

The platform is designed to:

* Transform financial news into structured market intelligence.
* Help analysts prioritize companies that deserve attention.
* Detect emerging opportunities and potential risks.
* Explain market behavior using transparent analytics.
* Provide business-focused insights instead of raw statistics.
* Automate repetitive analytical tasks.
* Support evidence-based decision making.

## Explainable Analytics

Every KPI should answer:

* Why did this score change?
* Which factors contributed most?
* What evidence supports the insight?

---

## Automation Engine

The platform automatically generates:

* Executive Market Brief
* Opportunity Alerts
* Risk Alerts
* Market Mood Updates
* Automated Insight Feed
* Daily Summary Reports
* Analyst Priority List

---

## Data Quality Layer

The platform continuously monitors:

* Duplicate news
* Missing values
* Coverage quality
* Source distribution
* Dataset completeness

---

## Confidence Layer

Every generated insight should include an estimated confidence level:

* High
* Medium
* Low

This helps users understand the reliability of each recommendation.

---

## Research Layer

The platform supports advanced analytics including:

* Correlation Analysis
* Lead-Lag Analysis
* Trend Analysis
* Comparative Company Analysis

Advanced statistical methods such as Granger Causality may be incorporated when sufficient historical data becomes available.

# Guiding Principles

Every feature must answer a business question.

Every visualization must support decision making.

Every KPI must be explainable.

Every insight must provide supporting evidence.

Every recommendation must be transparent.

Avoid unsupported prediction claims.

Prioritize analyst productivity over dashboard complexity.

Build a decision-support platform, not just another dashboard.

If a feature does not create measurable business value, it should not be included.
