# InsightPulse MVP Scope

## Goal

Build a professional Financial Market Decision Intelligence Platform that demonstrates strong Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, SQL, Python, and Power BI skills.

The project should prioritize quality, explainability, and business value over the number of features.

---

## Success Criteria

The MVP is considered complete only if it satisfies all of the following:

### Data Engineering

* Read operational data from AlphaLens.
* Create a separate analytics warehouse.
* Maintain a clean ETL pipeline.
* Ensure data quality checks.

### Analytics

* Generate reusable business metrics.
* Build explainable KPIs.
* Support trend analysis.
* Support comparative company analysis.

### Decision Intelligence

* Identify opportunities.
* Identify risks.
* Prioritize analyst attention.
* Generate supporting evidence.
* Assign confidence levels.

### Business Intelligence

* Professional star schema.
* Optimized Power BI model.
* Interactive dashboards.
* Drill-through capability.
* Cross-filtering.

### Automation

* Automated daily market brief.
* Executive insight generation.
* Recommendation engine.

### Documentation

* Professional README.
* Architecture documentation.
* KPI documentation.
* Data dictionary.

---

## Non-Goals

The MVP will NOT attempt to:

* Predict future stock prices.
* Guarantee investment performance.
* Replace professional financial advice.
* Use unnecessary AI or LLM features without measurable value.

---

## Definition of Done

InsightPulse is complete when a recruiter can:

1. Understand the business problem.
2. Understand the architecture.
3. Understand the data model.
4. See business-oriented KPIs.
5. Explore interactive dashboards.
6. Understand every recommendation through supporting evidence.
7. Recognize the project as a decision-support platform rather than a dashboard.
